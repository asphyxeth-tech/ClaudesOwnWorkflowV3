# Stream G — Fresh Hunt V4: Novel, AI-Native, Niche-Winning Concepts (2026-06-01)

## Why a reboot
Phases 0–1 + heavy concept hole-poking killed every "obvious" play. The pattern is not
bad luck — it's structural.

**REFRAME (hard-won lesson):** "Capture a real flow regardless of who shows up" = sitting
at a CHOKEPOINT. Every chokepoint worth having is already owned by a capitalized incumbent:
- Oracles / OEV-MEV recapture → **Chainlink** (SVR ~99% share across Aave/Compound/Venus;
  bought Atlas/FastLane Jan 2026) + UMA Oval / API3 / Pyth Express Relay / RedStone.
- Agent payments / guardrails → **Coinbase** (x402, custodial agent wallets).
- MEV extraction → pro searchers (latency + capital wars).
- Sequencing → rollup operators. Big launch flow → pump.fun / Clanker / Virtuals.

A <$5k newcomer cannot seize an owned chokepoint. So we STOP hunting "capture-flow-
regardless" pipes.

**NEW THESIS:** Build something *genuinely novel, with frontier AI at its core,* that a
*valuable niche actively chooses.* Win on product + novelty + AI quality, not on owning a
pipe. Relax constraint #1 from "zero adoption" to "**no MASS adoption required**" — a
worthwhile niche of high-value users / bots / protocols is enough.

## The kill-test (a candidate must survive ALL six)
1. **Crowding** — who already does it? Saturated → KILL.
2. **Chokepoint / ownability** — does an incumbent own the value capture? Can we win even a
   niche? No → KILL.
3. **AI-as-hero** — impossible or 10× worse without frontier AI? No → KILL.
4. **Money flow** — who pays, how much, is it proven ($)? Hypothetical → flag hard.
5. **Novelty / timing** — why not already saturated? why now?
6. **Buildable** — solo AI-assisted dev, <$5k, no capital deployment, substance-first
   provable in public, on-chain token that can accrue real revenue.

## THE REAL FINDING (the unlock)
Two of our constraints are **mutually exclusive for a newcomer**:
- **"Capture flow regardless of adoption"** = owning a chokepoint = REQUIRES capital
  (TVL/float/inventory) or a position incumbents already hold.
- **"<$5k, no capital deployed."**
You cannot have both — that is *why* every idea dies. So we must pick a lane:

- **Path α — keep "no capital," relax "capture-regardless":** build something people
  *choose*; win a niche on AI/product quality; accept that adoption is required. (No
  verified winner yet — obvious niches are crowded; needs a focused hunt for an
  underserved niche or a novel non-gambling consumer primitive.)
- **Path β — keep "structural flow capture," relax "<$5k":** put capital to work in an
  AI-edged position. Lead candidate: **AI-managed lending-vault curation** (Morpho-style:
  curator keeps ~100% of fees; ~$13M/yr pool, +600% YoY; AI = allocation/risk edge;
  earns on TVL even in flat markets). Catch: needs TVL/capital + competes with curator
  incumbents (Gauntlet/Steakhouse/Re7) → must target an underserved market.

## DECISION NEEDED
Bend capital (β) or adoption (α)? + what is the real capital picture? Then ONE focused,
high-conviction deep-dive in that lane — no more broad fan-out.
